The BGM 6 Zoning Amplifiers are designed with overall 70/100-volt or 4 to 16 ohms(Ω) loads and featured an industry leading 6- zoned 100 volt load solution, skillfully engineered into a 2U housing with rack mounting holes on both ends of front panel. 60/120/240/350/500 watts of amplifier power are available,with provided an audio output to 6 different zones within the venue, each zone has its own separate switch on/off manually operated on front panel. Like any other mixer amplifiers of ours,the 6 zoned amplifiers support two aux mono inputs,three local microphone inputs via 6.3mm interface,featuring muting function that MIC1 overrides MIC2,MIC3 AUX1,AUX2 with 0~30dB attenuation.
